💸 Welcome to Expense Tracker

This website is designed to help you manage your daily expenses in a simple and effective way. Many people spend money daily but do not keep track of where it goes. This leads to unnecessary spending and difficulty in saving money. Our Expense Tracker helps you solve this problem easily.

You can add your daily expenses like food, travel, shopping, and other needs. The website automatically calculates the total amount, so you can clearly understand your spending habits. By using this tool regularly, you can control your expenses and improve your financial planning.
